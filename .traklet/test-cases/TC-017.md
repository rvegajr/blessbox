{traklet:section:objective}
## Objective
A user can apply the WELCOME50 coupon code at checkout, see the total reduce by 50%, complete payment with Square sandbox card, and verify the discounted amount is charged.
{/traklet:section:objective}

{traklet:section:prerequisites}
## Prerequisites
- User signed in
- WELCOME50 coupon exists with 50% percentage discount
- Square sandbox configured
{/traklet:section:prerequisites}

{traklet:section:steps}
## Steps
1. Navigate to `/checkout?plan=standard`
2. Verify checkout shows $19.00
3. Enter coupon code: "WELCOME50"
4. Click "Apply Coupon"
5. Verify discount shown: "50% off"
6. Verify total updates to $9.50 ($19.00 × 50%)
7. Fill in Square sandbox test card:
   - Card: 4111 1111 1111 1111
   - CVV: 123
   - Expiry: 12/25
   - ZIP: 12345
8. Click "Complete Payment"
9. Verify payment processes successfully
10. Verify charged amount is $9.50 (not $19.00)
11. Verify redirect to dashboard
12. Check database: subscription shows discounted_amount = 9.50
{/traklet:section:steps}

{traklet:section:expected}
## Expected
- Discount calculates correctly: $19.00 × 50% = $9.50
- Square payment processes discounted amount
- Receipt shows original price, discount, and final amount
- Subscription record includes both base amount and discounted amount
- Coupon marked as used for this subscription
- User charged only $9.50 on sandbox card
{/traklet:section:expected}
