{traklet:section:objective}
## Objective
A member of the public can scan a QR code (or visit the registration URL directly), see the custom form configured by the organization, fill it out, submit successfully, and see confirmation.
{/traklet:section:objective}

{traklet:section:prerequisites}
## Prerequisites
- Organization has created QR codes (TC-004)
- Organization has configured registration form (TC-003)
- User is NOT signed in (anonymous public access)
{/traklet:section:prerequisites}

{traklet:section:steps}
## Steps
1. Scan QR code with phone camera OR navigate directly to `/register/{orgSlug}/{qrSlug}`
2. Verify registration form loads (status 200)
3. Verify form displays all configured fields in correct order
4. Verify required fields are marked with asterisk (*)
5. Fill in the form:
   - First Name: "John"
   - Last Name: "Doe"
   - Email: "john.doe@example.com"
   - Phone: "(555) 987-6543"
   - Fill any other custom fields
6. Click "Submit" or "Register" button
7. Verify success message appears
8. Verify redirect or confirmation page shown
9. Verify registration appears in admin dashboard
{/traklet:section:steps}

{traklet:section:expected}
## Expected
- Form loads without authentication (public access)
- All custom fields from form builder appear correctly
- Required field validation works (cannot submit with empty required fields)
- Email validation works (invalid email shows error)
- Phone validation works (invalid phone shows error)
- Successful submission creates registration record in database
- Registration linked to correct organization_id and qr_code_id
- QR code scan count increments by 1
- User sees thank you / success message
- Duplicate email handling (allow or warn based on org settings)
{/traklet:section:expected}
