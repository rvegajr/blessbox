{traklet:section:objective}
## Objective
A user can apply the FIRST10 coupon (fixed $10 discount) to Standard plan, see the total reduce from $19 to $9, and complete payment for the discounted amount.
{/traklet:section:objective}

{traklet:section:prerequisites}
## Prerequisites
- User signed in
- FIRST10 coupon exists with $10 fixed discount
{/traklet:section:prerequisites}

{traklet:section:steps}
## Steps
1. Navigate to `/checkout?plan=standard`
2. Enter coupon code: "FIRST10"
3. Click "Apply Coupon"
4. Verify discount shown: "$10.00 off"
5. Verify total updates to $9.00 ($19 - $10)
6. Complete payment with test card
7. Verify charged $9.00
8. Verify subscription active
{/traklet:section:steps}

{traklet:section:expected}
## Expected
- Discount calculates correctly: $19.00 - $10.00 = $9.00
- Total displays $9.00
- Payment processes for $9.00
- Subscription created with discounted_amount = 9.00
{/traklet:section:expected}
