{traklet:section:objective}
## Objective
A user can apply the SAVE20 coupon (fixed $20 discount) to Standard plan ($19), see the total cap at $1.00 minimum, and complete payment for the minimum amount.
{/traklet:section:objective}

{traklet:section:prerequisites}
## Prerequisites
- User signed in
- SAVE20 coupon exists with $20 fixed discount
- Minimum charge logic enforces $1.00 floor
{/traklet:section:prerequisites}

{traklet:section:steps}
## Steps
1. Navigate to `/checkout?plan=standard`
2. Enter coupon code: "SAVE20"
3. Click "Apply Coupon"
4. Verify discount shown: "$20.00 off"
5. Verify total updates to $1.00 (not $-1.00)
6. Verify message shown: "Minimum charge $1.00" or similar
7. Complete payment with test card
8. Verify charged $1.00
9. Verify subscription created successfully
{/traklet:section:steps}

{traklet:section:expected}
## Expected
- Discount would be $19 - $20 = -$1, but system enforces $1.00 minimum
- Total displays $1.00
- Payment processes for $1.00
- Subscription record shows original amount ($19), discount ($20), and charged amount ($1)
- No negative amounts charged or displayed
{/traklet:section:expected}
