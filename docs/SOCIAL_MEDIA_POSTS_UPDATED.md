## 💼 **LinkedIn**

```
Excited to announce a project I've been building! 🚀

I developed BlessBox (blessbox.org) - a QR-based registration and verification system designed specifically for:
• Food banks & distribution centers
• Churches & religious organizations
• Community nonprofits
• Volunteer coordination programs

The Problem I Wanted to Solve:
Organizations still rely on paper sign-up sheets, manual check-ins, and searching through lists. It's slow, error-prone, and creates bottlenecks.

How BlessBox Works:
1️⃣ Create customizable QR codes for different events/entry points
2️⃣ Attendees scan with their phone camera - no app required
3️⃣ They fill a simple registration form on their phone
4️⃣ System generates a unique QR code for check-in
5️⃣ Staff verifies instantly by scanning - no paper lists or manual lookup

Key Features:
✅ Free tier (100 registrations) - accessible for smaller orgs
✅ Tracks family size (critical for food distribution planning)
✅ Multiple entry point tracking
✅ Data export for reporting
✅ Works on any smartphone camera

Tech Stack: Next.js, TypeScript, Turso (SQLite), Square payments, Tailwind CSS

I'd love to hear your thoughts, and please share with anyone who might benefit!

🌐 blessbox.org
📧 support@blessbox.org

#BuildInPublic #SideProject #NonprofitTech #WebDevelopment #NextJS #TypeScript
```
