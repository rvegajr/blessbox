## How to Verify

### Option 1: Email Verification (Easiest)

1. **Check your email inbox** for `support@blessbox.org`
   - SendGrid should have sent a verification email
   - Look for email from SendGrid with subject like "Verify your sender identity"
   - Click the verification link in the email

2. **If you don't have access to `support@blessbox.org` inbox:**
   - You'll need to set up email forwarding first
   - OR verify via DNS (Option 2)

### Option 2: DNS Verification (If you control the domain)

1. **In SendGrid dashboard:**
   - Click the ellipsis (⋯) next to `support@blessbox.org`
   - Select "Verify" or "View DNS Records"
   - Copy the DNS records provided

2. **Add DNS records to your domain:**
   - Go to your domain registrar (where you manage `blessbox.org`)
   - Add the TXT records SendGrid provides
   - Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-60 minutes)

### Option 3: Resend Verification Email

1. **In SendGrid dashboard:**
   - Click the ellipsis (⋯) next to `support@blessbox.org`
   - Select "Resend Verification Email"
   - Check the inbox for `support@blessbox.org`

## Troubleshooting

**If verification email doesn't arrive:**
- Check spam folder
- Verify `support@blessbox.org` email account exists
- Try DNS verification instead

**If DNS verification fails:**
- Make sure TXT records are added correctly
- Wait longer for DNS propagation
- Check DNS records with: `dig TXT blessbox.org`
